💡 Recommendations

Production use: Hosted (Gemini 2.5 Flash) for quality-critical apps. 92% factual accuracy, superior hallucination resistance. Cost negligible at $0.30/M tokens.
Privacy-sensitive: OSS (Qwen2.5-0.5B) where data cannot leave device. 65% factual accuracy acceptable for casual conversation. Add RAG to reduce hallucinations.
Guardrails are essential: Safety middleware caught 50% of adversarial prompts at input layer. Without it, OSS model partially complied with indirect harmful requests.
Latency tradeoff: OSS on free CPU is 16x slower (23.8s vs 1.5s). For real-time chat, GPU ($0.40/hr) or hosted API strongly recommended.
